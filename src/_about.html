About me

I began learning code in my final year of university, at the beginning of 2022. I started with HTML5 and CSS3, successfully completing courses and assisted projects, and adapted SASS/SCSS as a CSS framework.


I grew comfortable of this very quick and moved onto learning JavaScript, using courses, assignments and challenges. Following theory, I began creating my own projects which cemented my knowledge and furthered my understanding of the language. Resolving bugs and bringing my own creative ideas to life helped me to understand the true functionality of JavaScript concepts and when best to use them.


Most recently, I have chosen jQuery as a JS library due it's popularity, and will continue to create projects and further perfect my JS skills.
